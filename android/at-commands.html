    Query basic info about device¶

    AST_POWERON

    Remove Lockscreen¶

    AT+SWATD=0
AT+ACTIVATE=0,0,0
AT+SWATD=1
AT+PARALLEL=2,0,00000;AT+DEBUGLVC=0,5
AT+PARALLEL=2,0,00000;AT+SERIALNO=2,
AT+FACTORST=0,0

    Remove FRP¶

    AT+KSTRINGB=0.3
AT+DUMPCTRL=1.0
AT+DEBUGLVC=0.5
AT+SWATD=0
AT+ACTIVATE=0,0,0
AT+SWATD=1
AT+DEBUGLVC=0.5

    Firmware download mode¶

    AT+SUDDLMOD=0,0
